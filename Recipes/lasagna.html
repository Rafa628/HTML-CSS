Spaghetti Bolognese

With this Spaghetti Bolognese recipe, the technique of the Italian classic has been simplified so you can create a tasty meal in under 30 minutes

This fast-track recipe for Quick & Easy Spaghetti Bolognese makes a meat sauce that’s bursting with Italian flavor and cooks in just 30 minutes!

Serve this easy meal with a warm loaf of Garlic Bread for the perfect Italian meal everyone will love!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 lb Spaghetti
  • 1 Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onions and sauté until lightly golden, 2 to 3 minutes. Add the garlic and fry another minute.
  2. Add the Ground Beef
  3. Fry until fully cooked and no pink shows at all in the meat. Then add the tomato paste and mix with the meat.
  4. Add the tomatoes, parsley, and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer 15 minutes. Serve with freshly cooked spaghetti.
